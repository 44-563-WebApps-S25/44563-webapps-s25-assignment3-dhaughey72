Day-old Pizza

I have lots of foods that I love, and my favorite food changes excedingly often, but one that is always a good option is day-old pizza. I find cold pizza the day after to be such a good comfort food. It's easy, low effort, and tastes arguably better than it did the day before!

How to prepare an oil painting:

  1. Make sure you have a canvas, a frame, mineral spirits, linseed oil, and oil paint.
  2. Start by making your canvas:
    1. Pull the fabric around the frame tightly and staple it all in the back.
    2. Staple the sides opposite from each other first.
  3. In a container, mix your linseed oil with a small amount of mineral spirits.
  4. You should then prep your canvas by painting
  5. You can now start your underpainting by dipping your brush in the paint and then the mixture before adding it to your canvas.
